    For the past 10 years I have been volunteering at an animal shelter. Helping those animals makes me feel truly alive. Not only has it been done in one place, but also has visited several shelters overseas to conduct a survey on the actual conditions. I have no doubt that I will spend my rest of my life for rescuing and protecting animals.
